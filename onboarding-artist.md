**SECTION 1 — WHAT IS PORTERFUL?**
Porterful is a direct-to-artist commerce platform. Every purchase on the platform — music, merch, or marketplace items — sends money straight to artists. No labels. No middlemen. No traditional gatekeepers.

**SECTION 2 — THE NUMBERS**
- Artists keep 80% of every sale
- Superfan Referrers earn 3% on sales they drive
- Porterful takes 10% to run the platform
- No monthly fees. No listing fees. You sell, you get paid.

**SECTION 3 — WHAT YOU CAN SELL**
- Music: albums, singles, beats — set your own price ($1 minimum)
- Merch: t-shirts, hoodies, prints via print-on-demand
- Digital: courses, PDFs, presets
- Marketplace: anything creative

**SECTION 4 — HOW SUPERFANS WORK**
Fans share your link. When someone buys, they earn a cut. It turns your supporters into promoters — and lets them build real income supporting artists they believe in.

**SECTION 5 — GETTING STARTED**
1. Sign up and verify as an artist
2. Upload your music or add products
3. Set your prices
4. Share your Porterful link
5. Get paid
