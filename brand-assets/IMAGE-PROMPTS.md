## Brand Imagery Guidelines

### DO:
- Black people in empowering, professional, joyful settings
- Warm lighting (orange, purple, golden)
- Modern, clean aesthetic — not corny or stereotypical
- Subtle money/wealth symbols (coins, flowing lines, circles)
- Community connection themes
- Music/culture elements for the music hub

### DON'T:
- Poverty imagery or "struggle" vibes
- Generic corporate stock look
- Cluttered or busy compositions
- Text or logos in the image
- Anything that looks low-res or cheap
