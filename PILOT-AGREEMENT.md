# PORTERFUL PILOT PROGRAM AGREEMENT

**Effective Date:** March 2026  
**Program:** Porterful Beta Testing Pilot

---

## 1. CONFIDENTIALITY

By participating in this Pilot Program, you agree that:

- All unreleased features, pricing structures, and platform functionality are **confidential**
- You will not share screenshots, recordings, or details publicly without written permission
- You will not reverse-engineer, scrape, or attempt to extract the platform's source code
- Internal calculations (artist splits, referral percentages) are proprietary trade secrets

**What's NOT confidential:**
- Publicly available pages (home page, public artist pages)
- Your own purchases and experience as a user
- General feedback you choose to share publicly (with permission)

---

## 2. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

- All feedback, suggestions, bug reports, and ideas you provide become **Porterful's property**
- Porterful has unlimited rights to use, implement, modify, or ignore your feedback
- You retain no ownership or credit for suggestions implemented
- You will not claim compensation for ideas contributed during testing

---

## 3. NON-COMPETE

During the Pilot Program and for **12 months after** your participation ends, you agree NOT to:

- Build, design, or contribute to a competing music/commerce platform
- "Competing platform" = any service selling music, merchandise, or digital content from artists directly to fans
- Work for or advise a company building similar direct-to-artist payment systems
- Use Porterful's data, artist lists, or user information for any competing purpose

**Exceptions:**
- You may work for Spotify, Apple Music, etc. in roles unrelated to artist direct-pay features
- General music industry work is permitted; targeting Porterful's model specifically is not

---

## 4. TESTING ENVIRONMENT

- This is a **beta** - features may break or change without notice
- Real transactions use real money and are binding
- You may encounter bugs; report them via the official Discord #bugs channel
- Porterful is not liable for inconveniences during testing

---

## 5. COMPENSATION

- You receive **early access** to Porterful features
- Pilot testers may receive perks (discounts, credits, recognition) at Porterful's discretion
- You will NOT receive:
  - Equity or ownership in Porterful
  - Monetary payment for testing
  - Guaranteed features or pricing

---

## 6. CONDUCT

You agree to:

- Test features in good faith
- Provide honest, constructive feedback
- Not intentionally break, exploit, or abuse the platform
- Not create fake accounts, transactions, or manipulate metrics
- Treat other testers and artists with respect in Discord channels

---

## 7. TERMINATION

Porterful may remove you from the Pilot Program at any time for:

- Violation of this agreement
- Abusive behavior toward staff or other testers
- Attempting to copy or replicate the platform
- Sharing confidential information publicly

Upon termination:
- You must delete any saved screenshots, recordings, or documentation
- Your non-compete obligation continues for 12 months
- You forfeit any pilot perks or early access

---

## 8. DATA & PRIVACY

- Your testing data, purchases, and activity may be logged for improvement purposes
- Your personal information is protected under Porterful's Privacy Policy
- You may request deletion of your pilot data at any time
