# Porterful Marketing Campaign — 2026

## The Moment
Independent artists are tired of:
- Spotify/Apple taking 70-80% of streaming revenue
- Bandcamp's complicated fee structure
- Shopify taking 2-3% per transaction + monthly fees
- TikTok's ever-changing monetization rules
- No ownership, no fan data, no control

Porterful is the anti-corporate platform. Built by artists, for artists.

---

## Core Messaging Pillars

### 1. "Own Everything"
- 80% of every sale goes to the artist
- No middleman. No label. No BS.
- Fan data belongs to the artist
- Direct relationships with fans

### 2. "The Platform IS Your Retirement"
- You're not just selling music — you're building equity
- Every upload, every sale, every fan = building something that pays you forever
- Traditional streaming won't be there when you're 60. Porterful will.

### 3. "From STL With Love"
- St. Louis roots. Real hip-hop culture.
- Not Silicon Valley. Not tech bros.
- We understand what it's like to hustle from the bottom up.
- Artists who came before us lit the path. We're building the tools they never had.

### 4. "Fans Win Too"
- Superfan program: fans who refer others EARN
- Not just passive listeners — active promoters with skin in the game
- Exclusive content, early access, community

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Emerging Hip-Hop/R&B Artists (18-35)
- 1K-50K followers
- Sick of streaming scraps
- Have a loyal core fanbase (even if small)
- Want to sell merch, not just streams

### Secondary: Independent Musicians (all genres)
- DIY artists tired of platform changes killing their income
- Artists with physical merch (shirts, vinyl, prints)

### Tertiary: Superfans (the promoters)
- Big listeners who share everything
- Currently get nothing for their advocacy
- Porterful makes them part of the team

## Campaign Ideas

### Phase 1: "The Leak" (Pre-Launch)
- Soft launch: 10-20 STL artists, free to onboard
- "Leak" Porterful to local scene via:
  - Instagram Stories / TikTok behind-the-scenes
  - Discord community
  - Word of mouth in STL music groups
- Collect waitlist signups
- Generate authentic buzz before spending a dollar on ads

### Phase 2: "Porterful Friday" (Launch)
- Every Friday, new music drops exclusively on Porterful before streaming
- Artists post "My track is live on Porterful first" content
- Fans get exclusive unreleased content, demos, behind-the-scenes
- Creates urgency and FOMO vs. just going to Spotify

### Phase 3: "The 80% Challenge"
- Artists who make $1,000+ on Porterful share their story
- "I made $1,347 on Porterful last month. On Spotify I made $47."
- User-generated content is the most powerful ads
- Create templates artists can remix with their own numbers

### Phase 4: "Superfan Showdown"
- Monthly competition: top referrer wins exclusive merch
- Leaderboard on site showing top promoters
- Gamifies the referral program
- Creates internal economy
