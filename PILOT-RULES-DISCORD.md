**Read this before joining. By entering, you agree:**

---

## 🔒 Keep It Private
- No screenshots outside this server
- No sharing unreleased features publicly
- This is beta - things will break

## 💡 Your Ideas Become Porterful's
- Feedback you give = we own it
- You won't get paid or credited for suggestions
- But you DO get early access and perks

## 🚫 No Copying
- For 12 months after testing, you won't build a competing music/commerce platform
- "Competing" = selling music/merch directly from artists to fans
- Working in music is fine; cloning Porterful is not

## ✅ Be Cool
- Report bugs in #bugs
- Be honest in feedback
- Don't abuse the platform or other testers

## ⚠️ Breaking Rules = Ban
- Violate this = removed immediately
- You'll lose pilot access and perks
- We take confidentiality seriously
